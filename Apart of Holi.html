The Best Colors

Colors associated with Holi include yellow, red, green, blue, pink, and brown. Yellow is chosen because it stands for the color of sunshine and is mixed with joy. Red because it is the color of fire, passion, and love. It is widely used in the Hindu world. Green is one of the colors because it is the color of nature, and it also symbolizes harmony and growth. The color blue represents not only the sky and the ocean but also stability and depth. Brown symbolizes stability, nature, warmth, and foundation.
